Steps to Play Audio and Video:

  1. Scroll through the webpage to locate the music and video section.
  2. The audio is set to default mode. Click the Play (▶) button on any audio track to start the music.
  3. Use the built-in controls on the audio player to adjust the volume or pause the music.
  4. To watch the video, click the Play (▶) button on the video player.
  5. You can also use full-screen mode by clicking the square icon at the bottom right of the video.
